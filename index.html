About Me

Riana Santatra Razafimamonjy is a dynamic junior web developer and versatile IT technician based in Antananarivo, Madagascar. With diverse experience ranging from freelance web integration to Level 1 and 2 IT support at Paositra Malagasy, he has built a solid technical foundation.
His web development expertise encompasses both front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (PHP, Python) technologies. Riana is proficient in frameworks such as Laravel, CodeIgniter, Django, and Bootstrap, along with MySQL databases. In addition, he has skills in IT maintenance, network management ans troubleshooting, and system administration for both Windows and Linux environments.
Riana holds a professional license in computer science, specializing in web design and development from Advancea, and a professional certificate in IT maintenance from CFPIG. He is recognized for his versatility, collaboration, problem-solving abilities, and intellectual curiosity.
