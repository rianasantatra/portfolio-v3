Riana Santatra Razafimamonjy is a dynamic junior web developer and versatile IT technician based in Antananarivo, Madagascar. With diverse experience ranging from freelance web integration to Level 1 and 2 IT support at Paositra Malagasy, he has built a solid technical foundation.

Riana is proficient in frameworks such as Laravel, CodeIgniter, Django, and Bootstrap, along with MySQL databases. Riana holds a professional license in computer science, specializing in web design and development from Advancea, and a professional certificate in IT maintenance from CFPIG. He is recognized for his versatility, collaboration, problem-solving abilities, and intellectual curiosity.
